Los Angeles, United States

Los Angeles is the city and seat of Los Angeles county of southern California, U.S. Los Angeles is the second most populated city in the United States. The city is found across a broad coastal plain. The city is between mountains and the Pacific Ocean. The city is where you can find the areas of Beverly Hills, Pasadena, and Long Beach. This is also where Channel Islands, Santa Catalina and San Clemente are found.

Los Angeles is the central of southern Califonia. Just recently, it became a world-class city. During the 20th century, it was just considered as a "large village". It overcame it's natural lacks and became an establishment of commerce, agriculture, tourism, and industry. It became known to be associated to good weather, leisure and recreation, and especially the aura of Hollywood. However, it does have exceptions. Los Angeles has been a place of ethnic and racial diversity, largely due to immigration, and shows a growing gap between rich and poor.

New York, United States

New York City is the largest and most influential city in the United States. This city covers Manhattan and Staten islands, the western sections of Long Island, and a part of the New York state mainland to the north of Manhattan.

New York City composes of many neighbourhoods. This is known as the fire boroughs. This includes Manhattan, Brooklyn, the Bronx, Queens, and Staten Island.

During the end of the 20th century, there were roughly 200 national groups in the city. This includes European ancestry, Hispanics, African Americans, and Asians, which is now the fastest-growing component of the population. The Statue of Liberty continues to symbolize enligtenment, as it welcomes immigrants into the city's golden door.

Toronto, Canada

Toronto is the city and capital of the province of Ontario, Canada. Toronto is the most populated city in Canada. It is a multicultural city, and considered as the country's commercial and financial centre.

Toronto has a continental climate. Due to its distance from the Great Lakes, this changes. During winter, average temperature is in the low to mid-20s F (about –4.2 °C), but wind chill may lower this even more. In the summer months, temperature can go from low 70s F (about 22.2 °C) to 90 °F (32 °C). The Great Lakes also affects precipitation which can cause it to rain all year round.

Toronto is a cultural centre. There are plenty of musical groups that are known internationally. For example, the Toronto Symphony Orchestra. There are also many major theatres in the area including Factory Theatre. There are also plenty of museums, such as the The Art Gallery of Ontario and the Royal Ontario Museum. Different attraction destinations also exist in this city such as the Ontario Science Centre, which holds plenty of hands-on exhibists, and Ontario Place, which holds the Canadian National Exhibition yearly.
