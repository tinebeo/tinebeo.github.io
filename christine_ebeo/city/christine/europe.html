London, United Kingdom

London is the city capital of the United Kingdom. London is one of the oldest great cities of the world. London is also the country’s economic, transportation, and cultural centre.

London has about a third of the country's art galleries. Some of the greatest collections is the National Gallery. This is found in the Trafalgar Square. Behind this is where one can find the National Portrait Gallery. This is place has a large collection of paintings, drawings, sculptures, etchings, photographs, and miniatures of famous faces past and present.

People here are big fans of football. Football is at the top of the list, both in participang and spectator sport in London. You will see hundreds of amateur players for games in every park and open space. London has some dozen football clubs. This including Arsenal, Chelsea, Tottenham Hotspur, and West Ham United.

Paris, France

Paris is the city and capital of France. Paris is located in the north-central part of the France.

For several centuries, Paris has been the cultural caputal of the Western world. It has been a known to many artists, intellectuals, and a place where new ideas originate. Art also reign supreme. This was most expecially true during the 20th century. This wa during the time when numerous writers, artists, including Ernest Hemingway from the United States, James Joyce from Ireland, Pablo Picasso from Spain, and Amedeo Modigliani from Italy.

While there those who critic the city and have said that Parisian culture is more for show instead of showing true creativity, the city's cultural life is still alive and distinctive. People here love novelty. They have an great intellect, elegance, which makes places like theatres, concert halls, museums, art galleries, and art cinemas well attended.

Madrid, Spain

Madrid is a city and capital of Spain. Madrid is also, Spain’s arts and financial centre. It is considered to be the city proper and has formed an autonomous community in central Spain.

Madrid has many attractions. It has the largest bullring in Spain, known as the Las Ventas. This place as a capacity of 25,000 people and is the place where novice bullfighters have to display their skills in the alternativa. This is the event where a matador kills his first bull. Bullfighting lasts from March to October.

Football is also a big sport here. There are two major football teams in Madrid known as the Real Madrid and Club Atlético de Madrid. Some of the important football event is the annual matches against the Barcelona squads.
